Feature Highlights

One clean operating system for selling and managing jobs.

Built around the way contractors actually work: quote fast, get approval, schedule the install, document the project, and track the money.

01

Professional Quotes

Create polished proposals with rooms, line items, taxes, deposits, notes, and a live total that stays visible while you build.

02

Digital Signatures

Customers can review and sign from their phone so approvals do not get buried in texts, paper, or email threads.

03

Scheduling

Turn sold quotes into scheduled jobs with install dates, duration, crew, notes, status, and quick customer actions.

04

Google Calendar

Generate calendar links and downloadable events so jobs can move cleanly into the calendar tools your crew already uses.

05

Job File

Open a premium internal job file from the schedule with contact actions, money snapshot, scope details, photos, notes, and job status.

06

Photo Timeline

Group project photos by estimate, pre-install condition, installation progress, and finished project so every job tells a visual story.

07

Activity Timeline

Keep a permanent chronological history of quote approvals, payments, schedule changes, status updates, and manual job notes.

08

Money Tracker

Track deposits, paid-to-date, balances, and payment history without switching away from the job workflow.

09

Customer Database

Keep customer contact details, quote history, scheduled work, and project context organized in one searchable place.
